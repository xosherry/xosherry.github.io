KOREA, SEPT. 2017: Myeongdong

Myeongdong is a good shopping district for makeup, colored contacts, and super cute cheap clothing. Shopping seems like a very popular daily activity in Seoul, which explains why every one dresses so nice. Grab some yummy street food and take a walk down the streets. You will find your hands filled with free face masks, shampoo samples, and lotions!

Speaking of face masks, there's always 10+10 deals in Seoul - meaning, pay $10 USD to get 20 face masks. Additionally, colored contacts were about two pairs for $20-$30. Prescription glasses range from $20 - $120, and there's so much nice affordable makeup at stores like Stylenada (pink shelves pictured below) too! Definitely get gifts here for yourself and loved ones back home.

Our first night in Seoul, trying to blend in with these shoppers.

Some things I did in Myeongdong:

  • Street food:
    • Tteokbokki (rice cakes): 10/10 recommend
    • Bungeoppang Honey Ice Cream: 4/10 I didn't get the hype for some honey topped ice cream wrapped in bread but that's just me =)


  • Stores:
    • Stylenanda Pink Hotel: 10/10 All the makeup is sample-able, good quality, and this place is so instagrammable.
    • Innisfree: 10/10 Okay so this beauty store is every-freakin-where, but it makes you realize how hard Sephora has finessed our wallets.
